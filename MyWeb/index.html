Projects

Cyclodextrin Metal Organic Framework 1 (CD-MOF-1) on the Rate of Carbon Dioxide Absorption

Tested the efficiency of CD-MOF-1 with multiple amines on carbon dioxide absorption. CD-MOF-1 helps capture carbon dioxide by trapping it in it's porous structures. Amines help reduce carbon dioxide to carbonate by acting as a base on water (Amine + 2H2O + CO2 → Amine+ + H3O+ + CO3-). Used MDEA (N-Methyldiethanolamine), 1,6-Hexanediamine, and EDTA (Ethylenediamine triacetic acid). Found that EDTA performed best and that CD-MOF-1 lowered efficiency when combined with amines. Though, trials were limited and more research should be done. CREDIT: TJHSST Chemistry Research Lab

Polarization Dependent Photoelectrochemistry of Anisotropic Rhenium Disulfide

Worked with conducting Rhenium Disulfide, a member of the transition metal dichalcogenides (TMDs) family. These two-dimensional materials have high surface area and tunability, which make them excellent photocatalysts. ReS2, especially, has a rhenium diamond shaped structure which changes depending on the axis viewed. Tested the different axes of ReS2 when shined with light using scanning electrochemical cell microscopy (SECCM). Found that maximum current was achieved when the light's electric field aligned with the rhenium diamond structure's long end (the b-axis). The more ReS2 layers stacked, the less of a difference the alignment made. CREDIT: GMU Chemistry Department Research Lab; More information visit: the GMU ASSIP journal

Application of Zinc Copper Indium Sulfide Quantum Dots (ZCIS QDs) in Solar Cells

Created different sized ZCIS QDs and measured their efficiency against different colors of light. Different sized QDs were created by extracting them at different time intervals. Darker QDs (more red) were taken out later than lighter ones (more yellow). Ran fluorescence spectroscopy using 420 nm excitation value to determine peak wavelength and band gap of each QD, then found radius. Tested different colors of light using sticky notes on top of a constant light source. Found that middle-sized QDs absorbed light the best. All QDs showed that they absorb more purple light versus other colors. In addition, white light was absorbed the best for all QDs. CREDIT: TJHSST Chemistry Research Lab Senior Research
